The Rules of Hex

  • Two players (red and blue) take turns placing stones.
  • Red owns the top and bottom edges. Blue owns the left and right sides.
  • The player who connects his or her two sides with an unbroken chain wins.
  • Red goes first. After red's first move, the second player may choose to swap colors. If so, the first player, now blue, immediately plays again. (The swap rule neutralizes first-player advantage.)

For more info and strategies, see Hex Wiki.
